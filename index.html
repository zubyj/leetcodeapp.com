Frequently Asked Questions

Leetcode Explained is a free and open source browser extension that adds company tags, solution videos, and GPT code analysis into Leetcode problems.

With Leetcode Explained, you can access video solutions directly on Leetcode problem pages, identify errors in your code, and understand the complexity of your solution. It's an efficient tool for learning and improving your coding abilities.

You can install Leetcode Explained from the Chrome Web Store. Simply visit the extension's page and click the "Add to Chrome" button to begin using it.

Yes, Leetcode Explained is entirely free to use. We believe in providing quality resources that can aid in improving coding skills, and we are delighted to offer this to our users free of charge.
